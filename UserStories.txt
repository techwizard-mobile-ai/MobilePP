User Stories 
1.) As a homeowner, I would like the product to alert me to any fires in my house. It must be reliable and quick for the product to be useful.

2.) As a landlord, I would like the product to quickly and accurately detect any smoke in my apartments for a variety of reasons.

3.) As a gardener, I would like to monitor the internal air quality (Temperature/Pressure/Humidity) of my greenhouse. The products readings must be consistent and accurate. 

4.) As a homeowner, I would like to monitor any potentially dangerous gas leaks that could be occuring in my home. Readings must be accurate and notify me immediately.

5.) As a consumer, I would like to view past and present air quality statistics, in order to observe trends of air quality in my home.
